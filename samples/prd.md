# Product Requirements Document

## 1. Executive Summary

Our lending platform is an online portal for individuals to apply for personal loans and upload required documents. The platform aims to provide a user-friendly experience, similar to existing banking platforms, while targeting individuals with any credit history.

## 2. Overview

* The platform will be built as a self-contained website, without integration with the bank's existing systems.
* The platform will use targeted marketing campaigns via email to identify potential prospects based on their profile and behavior.

## 3. Functional Requirements

* User-friendly interface for customers to submit loan applications and upload required documents
* Targeted marketing campaigns via email to identify potential prospects
* Secure online form for customer data submission
* Document upload feature with secure storage
* Personalized experience for customers

## 4. Data Protection and Regulation Compliance

* Customer data will be stored securely, both in transit and at rest.
* Personal data processing will comply with GDPR regulations.
* Sensitive information will be handled securely and in accordance with relevant regulations.
* Data minimization will be implemented to ensure only necessary data is collected and processed.

## 5. Regulation Compliance

* The platform will comply with GDPR regulations for personal data processing.
* KYC/AML policies will be implemented to verify customer identity and prevent money laundering.
* A data retention policy will be established to ensure minimum necessary data is retained.
* A plan for notifying customers in the event of a data breach will be developed.

## 6. Security Measures

* Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) Certificate implementation
* Two-Factor Authentication implementation
* Data Encryption using HTTPS

## 7. Success Metrics and KPIs

* Number of successful loan applications processed per month
* Customer retention rate after first loan application
* Average loan amount disbursed to customers

## 8. Assumptions and Dependencies

* Assumption: Existing bank systems will not be integrated with the platform.
* Dependency: Development team's ability to implement secure online forms and document upload features.

## 9. Open Questions and Uncertainties

* How will customer data be verified and validated?
* What are the specific requirements for KYC/AML policies?

## 10. Glossary

* Customer Data: Personal and sensitive information collected from customers
* GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation
* KYC: Know Your Customer
* AML: Anti-Money Laundering
